Voices of India

A student-led interview magazine capturing the diverse perspectives of everyday Indians on development, identity, and cultural change.

Magazine Cover

Project Overview

"Voices of India" is a grassroots interview magazine that I created to explore how India's fast-paced development is being experienced by people from all walks of life — from auto drivers and shopkeepers to educators and students.

Over the course of several months, I interviewed 15 individuals across different backgrounds to hear their raw, honest thoughts on changing values, digital shifts, education, aspirations, and how they envision the future of India.

Themes Explored

Urban vs Rural Growth

Contrasting hopes and challenges between metro cities and villages.

Cultural Evolution

How family, festivals, and traditions are evolving with modern India.

Digital India

Perspectives on internet penetration, smartphones, and social media.

Dreams & Reality

What development really means to people at the ground level.

What's Next?

I’m currently designing the digital magazine layout and plan to publish both a PDF version and an interactive website for readers to browse, reflect, and even submit their own stories.

My goal is to eventually scale this into a crowdsourced civic journal that highlights India through real voices — not just headlines.
