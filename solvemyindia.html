Project Overview

SolveMyIndia is a civic-tech platform I conceptualized to give voice to Indian citizens, especially students and young innovators. Users can post local and national problems, suggest creative solutions, and vote on ideas that matter most.

Built with a strong mission of participatory democracy, the platform features real-time discussion, issue tagging, and a transparent voting mechanism — all designed to encourage youth-led change and problem-solving at scale.

Key Features

Idea Submission

Citizens submit real-life problems or innovative ideas in areas like waste, infrastructure, health, and tech.

Democratic Voting

Real-time voting system to prioritize the most impactful and supported ideas by the community.

Discussion Forums

Users engage in public discussions, refining ideas through feedback and collaborative suggestions.

Youth Spotlight

Special section to feature student-led solutions from rural and urban areas across India.

Technical Stack

SolveMyIndia is a scalable MERN-based web platform built to handle real-time updates, voting, and community forums. It’s also optimized for regional accessibility to ensure inclusivity across India.

Technologies Used

Results & Vision

SolveMyIndia is still in its MVP phase, but has already attracted attention from peers, educators, and social workers. The platform aims to onboard 5,000+ users, especially students from rural India during my gap year outreach.

This is more than a website — it's a movement. Through public feedback loops, NGO collaboration, and tech-driven solutions, SolveMyIndia will grow into a national youth voice platform for change.
