Project Overview

Avoneme is my creative agency startup built during high school, aiming to empower businesses and communities through digital innovation. We specialize in website development, UI/UX design, and professional video editing.

As the Founder and Creative Lead, I directed the studio’s vision, managed end-to-end design systems, and led client project execution. Avoneme blends technical execution with aesthetic precision—making professional design accessible to emerging startups and NGOs.
